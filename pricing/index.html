What You Get

Dedicated resources with full management included

Lite Container

$79/year

Ultra-lightweight Alpine Linux LXC container on Proxmox VE. Perfect for static sites, small WordPress installations, or email-only hosting. Shares the host kernel for maximum efficiency with 1GB RAM and 10GB NVMe storage. Includes Nginx, PHP-FPM, MariaDB, Postfix/Dovecot mail stack, and automatic SSL via Let's Encrypt.

Small VM

$149/year

Full Debian 12 virtual machine with dedicated kernel and complete isolation. 2GB RAM and 20GB NVMe storage. Ideal for growing WordPress sites, small web applications, or businesses needing dedicated resources. Includes the complete multi-service stack: Postfix/Dovecot/SpamProbe for email, Nginx/PHP-FPM for web, and MariaDB for databases.

Medium VM

$199/year

Production-ready Debian 12 VM with 4GB RAM and 40GB NVMe. Suitable for busier WordPress sites with WooCommerce, multiple domains, or applications with moderate traffic. Full root access available on request. All updates, security patches, and backups handled for you.

Large VM

$249/year

High-performance Debian 12 VM with 8GB RAM and 80GB NVMe storage. Built for demanding applications, high-traffic sites, or businesses consolidating multiple services. Ample headroom for caching, large databases, and concurrent users. Includes all managed services plus priority support.

Managed BYO

$120/year + your VPS cost

Already have a VPS at BinaryLane, Vultr, DigitalOcean, or elsewhere? We'll take over management. You pay your provider directly for the VPS, and we handle all the Linux administration: OS updates, security hardening, service configuration, monitoring, backups, and troubleshooting. Perfect for developers who want to focus on code, not sysadmin.

Scale As You Grow

Flexible add-ons

Need more resources? Add RAM, storage, or dedicated IPs at any time without migrating. Each add-on is just $20/year: +1GB RAM for memory-hungry apps, +10GB NVMe for growing databases, or +1 dedicated IP for SSL certificates or mail reputation. Upgrades are applied within 24 hours.
